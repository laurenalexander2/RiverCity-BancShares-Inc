Executive Vice President, Strategic Initiatives & Affordable Mortgage Finance Division President

Marcus Daryl Adams

    22-year Housing Expert and Banker Marcus Adams works to strengthen relationships with community groups, small businesses, and non-profit organizations. In his previous roles he worked with business start-ups, community-based organizations, and faith-based groups to achieve solutions for their capital requirement needs.
    Adams is a certified HUD housing counselor and originally brought NACA (Neighborhood Assistance Corporation of America) to St. Louis. Adams directed this office for 9 years.
  • BA, Music Management University of Southern Mississippi
  • MBA-Keller Graduate School of Management, DeVry University
  • Certified Housing Counseling Manager-NCRC
  • Community Advisory Council Chair-Community Action Agency of St. Louis County
  • Homebuyer Instructor-Beyond Housing
  • AmeriCorps Alum
