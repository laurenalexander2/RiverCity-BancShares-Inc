BOARD DIRECTOR

Cynthia McCafferty

    Cynthia McCafferty joined Hawthorne Strategy Group as President & CEO in 2015, after two decades at Fleishman Hillard International Communications in St. Louis, where she served as a senior vice president and senior partner. At Fleishman Hillard, McCafferty became an expert in strategy and integrated campaign management. and was Senior Vice President and Senior Partner at St. Louis based Fleishman Hillard. McCafferty co-led Get Covered Illinois prior to joining Hawthorne. During her 18 years at Fleishman Hillard, McCafferty led strategic communications efforts for such clients as Home Instead Senior Care, Ascension Health, and Missouri Foundation for Health.
    McCafferty has a bachelor’s degree in American Studies from Saint Louis University and MBA from Washington University in St. Louis. She began her career as an environmental grassroots organizer and worked for the Missouri Democratic Party, in addition to ­­­ political action committees and candidates. McCafferty currently serves on the YWCA of Chicago Board of Directors and previous board of FOCUS St Louis.
    In 2019, McCafferty was recognized as one of “Chicago’s Most Influential Women” by the Chicago Business Journal and 2009’s “40 under 40” by the Saint Louis Business Journal.
