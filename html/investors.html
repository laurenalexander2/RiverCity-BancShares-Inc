Our Big Idea

  • Acquire a small Community Bank and rebrand it as a Community Development Bank.
  • Specialize in Small Business Financing that will expand Credit to a larger populace, partially aided by a Community Development Division.
  • Affordable Mortgage Financing with specialty products through a Social Venture.

    • Our Business Model:

    RiverCity will be comprised of a Community Bank and an Affordable Mortgage Division.

    • Community Bank: Woman and Black-Owned, Southwest Model.
    • Affordable Mortgage Finance Division: Certified Community Housing Development Organization, Headed by M. Daryl Adams.
